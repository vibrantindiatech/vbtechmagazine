"The Sourcing Advantage: Trends and Takeaways from Vibrant India"

A Comprehensive Insight into India’s Premier Houseware & Home Appliances Trade Fair, Highlighting Key Trends, Product Segments, and Exclusive B2B Opportunities.

The Vibrant India Fair is the definitive annual marketplace where domestic and international B2B chains converge. Attracting over 800 exhibitors and 40,000 trade visitors, it sets the purchasing trends for the next financial year.The fair showcases India's robust manufacturing capabilities, particularly in stainless steel and plasticware. For wholesalers and bulk buyers, sourcing directly from over 500 manufacturers under one roof means better price discovery and streamlined logistics.

Event Scale and Comprehensive Product Segments

As India's largest B2B exhibition for houseware and home appliances, the Vibrant India Fair consistently attracts over 800 brands and 38,000–40,000 trade visitors, covering the entire supply chain from raw materials to finished products.

16 Core Exhibition Segments

The fair is divided into 16 segments for efficient sourcing and networking. Buyers can easily navigate to find products spanning domestic and international demands:
